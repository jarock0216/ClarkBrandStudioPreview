About Me

Welcome to Clark Brand Studios, where moments become memories, energy becomes art, and every visual tells a story.

My name is Lettie Clark, founder and creative force behind Clark Brand Studios, a multimedia brand built on passion, creativity, and capturing life in real time. I’m a videographer, photographer, and content creator based in Greensboro, North Carolina, specializing in visuals that feel authentic, cinematic, and unforgettable.

I believe the best moments happen naturally, the laughs, the crowd reactions, the confidence, the emotions, the energy in the room, and the memories people don’t even realize are being created while they’re living in the moment. That’s what I love to capture.

What started as a passion for storytelling through a camera turned into a brand known for crisp visuals, cinematic edits, and content that helps businesses, brands, events, and individuals stand out.

From nightlife events and packed-out karaoke nights to branding shoots, graduations, celebrations, and promotional campaigns, my goal is always the same: create visuals that make people feel something long after the moment is over.

At Clark Brand Studios, I don’t believe in generic content. Every project is approached with creativity, attention to detail, and the mindset of creating something timeless. I don’t just record events, I capture the vibe, the atmosphere, and the emotion behind every experience.

Over the years, I’ve had the opportunity to work with entrepreneurs, creatives, performers, event hosts, and everyday people looking to bring their vision to life. Whether I’m behind the camera documenting a major event, creating promo content for a business, or capturing milestone moments for families and graduates, I approach every project with professionalism, vision, and passion.

What makes my work different is the energy behind it. My style blends clean visuals, real emotion, vibrant culture, and modern storytelling to produce content that feels alive.
